Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

At Savory Meal Journal, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe digital environment for all our visitors. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the security protocols we employ to protect your data.

For any inquiries regarding this policy or data processing, please contact our data team at privacy@savorymealjournal.com.

1. Information Categories Collected

We process different types of information for distinct operational needs:

  • Personal Identifiable Data: Collected when you fill out consultation forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or submit collaborations. This includes your name, email, company name, phone number, and physical mailing addresses.
  • Usage & Device Analytics: Collected automatically through web logs. This features your IP address, browser type, device specs, pages visited, time stamps, and referral links.
  • Tracking & Cookie Data: Sourced via cookies and beacons to monitor interaction and remember your preferences to deliver premium layout displays.

2. Data Utilization

Savory Meal Journal processes collected data to:

  • Provide, run, and optimize our digital layout and agency services.
  • Understand and analyze user interaction to expand our creative portfolio.
  • Communicate project updates, newsletters, and promotional styling guides.
  • Serve relevant, non-intrusive ads through platforms like Google Ads.
  • Identify and prevent system vulnerabilities, unauthorized access, or fraudulent transactions.

3. Log File Analysis

Savory Meal Journal follows industry standards by maintaining log files. These files document visitor traffic when accessing the site. This is a default practice of hosting servers and forms part of hosting analytics. Information includes IP addresses, browser client, ISP details, date stamps, and referring/exit coordinates. This data is fully aggregated and is not linked to any personally identifiable information, serving only to monitor demographic layout and web performance.

4. Third-Party Advertising & Tracking Policies

We work with trusted marketing partners, including Google Ads, who serve cookies and web beacons on our site. These third-party tracking services collect aggregated data to analyze marketing performance and serve personalized advertising based on your previous browsing history.

Please note that Savory Meal Journal does not have direct access or control over cookies set by third-party ad networks. We encourage you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third parties for detailed instructions on how to opt-out.

5. Data Security

We maintain appropriate physical, electronic, and administrative procedures to secure user data from loss, misuse, or alteration. However, please remember that no web transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data protection.

6. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you possess specific data protection rights, including: the right to access stored data, the right to edit inaccuracies, the right to request deletion (the right to be forgotten), the right to object to data processing, and the right to port your data. To exercise these rights, please email us directly.
