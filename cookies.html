Cookies Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how Savory Meal Journal uses cookies, web beacons, and tracking technologies when you access our digital recipe journal. By continuing to browse, you consent to our configuration of cookies as outlined below.

1. What are Web Cookies?

Cookies are minor text records placed on your client browser when you visit a website. They serve to recognize user devices, facilitate correct page loading, remember preference variables, and analyze marketing traffic.

First-party cookies are set directly by Savory Meal Journal. Third-party cookies are set by partner services (e.g. Google Ads and analytics modules) to provide external features and targeting codes.

2. Cookie Classifications Employed

We deploy cookies under the following structural categories:

  • Strictly Necessary/Essential: Vital for basic system operations, navigation grids, and session integrity. The website will not operate correctly without these.
  • Performance & Web Metrics: Aggregate anonymous data on page speed, most-visited sections, and interface interactions (e.g. Google Analytics). This allows us to continuously optimize layout performance.
  • Targeting & Advertising: Used by networks (like Google Ads) to deliver relevant ad placements based on your interests and analyze marketing campaign effectiveness.

3. Managing Cookie Preferences

You can choose to accept or reject cookies. When you visit our website for the first time, our Cookie Consent Banner allows you to select your settings. You can also modify your browser controls to decline cookies, although some website functionalities may be restricted as a result.

4. Policy Updates

We update this Cookies Policy periodically to align with shifting web technologies or regulatory changes. We encourage users to check this page regularly to stay informed about our tracking methodologies.
